ABOUT US

Book planet is a platform where users can buy and sell books online. Through Book Planet the users can search for a book by its title or author, later can add to the shopping cart and finally purchase using online transaction. The user can also sell books at a price fixed by him/her. Books to be sold are uploaded by entering the price,title , description with an image of the book attatched. The user can login using his account details or new customers can set up an account very quickly.
